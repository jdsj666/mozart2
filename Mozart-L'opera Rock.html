Mozart l'opéra rock

, translated as "Mozart the Rock Opera", is a French pop musical that garnered immense popularity in 2009. It is a French-language rock musical that depicts the life of the musician Mozart. The combination of bel canto and pop, the crossover between rock and classical, along with a cast of visually striking and vocally talented actors, have all contributed to the play's global appeal. In 2009, the French pop musical "Mozart l'opéra rock" charted for 20 weeks, peaking at number three and ranking ninth the previous week.

Mozart

, a musical genius who was not satisfied with the constraints of tradition, left his hometown of Salzburg and embarked on a journey to find musical freedom. He encounters a setback in Mannheim, but meets the Webb sisters and falls into a love entanglement. After that, Mozart went to Paris, but his career and love did not go well, and his mother died in Paris. After returning to Salzburg, Mozart's life hit a low point, but in Vienna he got a chance to perform and began to make his mark. He composed works such as "The Harem Escape", "The Marriage of Figaro" and "Don Giovanni", but was ostracized by the aristocracy and suppressed by court musicians.
